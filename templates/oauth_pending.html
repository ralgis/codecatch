Manual flow (because Mozilla's client_id only registered an embedded-webview redirect URI):
  1. Click Open consent on a row below. A new tab opens with Microsoft login.
  2. Sign in with the mailbox password. If Microsoft prompts for an alternate email, enter codes.social@gmx.de (the recovery email); the verification code will arrive in codecatch and you can see it on the Codes page.
  3. Click Allow on the consent screen.
  4. You'll land on a page that says "This page is not normally shown and could be a sign of a phishing attempt" — that's our page. The URL contains ?code=.... Don't close the tab.
  5. Copy the entire URL from the address bar (Ctrl+L, Ctrl+C) and paste it into the form at the bottom of this page.
